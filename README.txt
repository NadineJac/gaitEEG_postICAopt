data

- spider_plot from https://de.mathworks.com/matlabcentral/fileexchange/59561-spider_plot
- brainstorm https://neuroimage.usc.edu/bst/download.php (functions morlet_wavelet.m, morlet_wavelet_fast.m and cmap_manddrill.m are used)
- gaitEEG_footprint from https://github.com/NadineJac/gaitEEGfootprint
